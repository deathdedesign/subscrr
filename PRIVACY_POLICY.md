Privacy Policy for Subscrr
Last updated: 2026-01-30

Subscrr helps you track subscriptions. This policy explains what data we handle and why.

Summary
- Account: Sign in with Apple is used to authenticate you.
- Storage: Your subscriptions are stored in your iCloud private database via CloudKit.
- We do not sell your data and do not use third‑party analytics or advertising SDKs.

Data We Collect
1) Sign in with Apple
   - Apple user identifier (a unique, non‑public identifier).
   - Name and email, only if you choose to share them with us.

2) App content you create
   - Subscription details you enter (name, price, renewal period, dates, category, notes, icons, colors).

Where Your Data Is Stored
- Subscription data is stored in your iCloud private database using CloudKit. It is associated with your iCloud account and synced across your devices.
- The Apple user identifier is stored locally in the iOS Keychain to keep you signed in.

How We Use Data
- To authenticate you with Sign in with Apple.
- To save and sync your subscriptions across devices.
- To provide reminders and statistics inside the app.

Data Sharing
- We do not share your personal data with third parties.
- Apple provides the Sign in with Apple and iCloud services that enable authentication and sync.

Data Retention
- Your subscription data remains in your iCloud private database until you delete it or remove the app’s iCloud data from your account.
- The Apple user identifier stored in Keychain is removed when you sign out.

Your Choices
- You can choose to hide your email with Sign in with Apple.
- You can delete subscriptions at any time.
- You can request deletion by removing the app’s iCloud data from your Apple ID settings.

Security
We use Apple’s secure services (Sign in with Apple, Keychain, and CloudKit) to protect your data.

Children’s Privacy
Subscrr is not intended for children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect data from children.
